Porto Alegre

About the Temple

The Porto Alegre Brazil Temple is the 102nd operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church). It is located in the Brazilian city of Porto Alegre, capital of the state of Rio Grande do Sul. The temple serves approximately 137,500 members in the area.

Dedicated 17 Dez 2000
